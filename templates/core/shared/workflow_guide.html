{% if user.role == "admin" %}
Full Workflow Overview
Reflective reports move through Draft, Submitted, Reviewed, Approved, and Returned. Work diaries move through Draft, Submitted, Reviewed, Approved, and Returned. Volunteer transcripts move through Draft, Pending Review, Approved, and Returned to Draft. Admin manages setup, oversight, transcript visibility, and exports.
Administration Setup
  1. Create users and assign one or more roles from User Role Manager.
  2. Maintain configuration masters such as schools, locations, chapters, programmes, statuses, session types, and rating domains.
  3. Import mentees, review upload logs, and confirm mentor or endorser assignments.
  4. Use the admin dashboard, exports, notifications, and monitoring tools for platform governance.
Volunteer Reporting
  1. Volunteers create work diaries with date, duration, location, narrative, and optional evidence.
  2. Volunteers submit reflective reports with programme, location, endorser, learnings, feedback, suggestions, and photo evidence.
  3. Draft entries stay editable until resubmitted.
  4. Returned items are revised by volunteers and sent back into review.
Review and Final Approval
  1. Mentors and endorsers handle the review stage through Review Dashboard and Review Queue.
  2. Only submitted reflective reports, submitted work diaries, and pending transcripts appear in review lists.
  3. Admin performs final approval for reflective reports and work diaries after mentor or endorser review is complete.
  4. Each decision is tracked in the approval log and related notifications are issued automatically.
Transcript Process
  1. Volunteers generate transcript drafts from approved reports, approved diaries, activities, profile artifacts, and linked evidence.
  2. Transcript drafts move from Pending Review to Approved once a mentor or endorser completes the review.
  3. Admin can monitor transcript status and download the export file after reviewer approval.
  4. Approved transcripts generate a downloadable export file.
Mentee Development
  1. Mentees maintain objectives, year plans, and assessment history.
  2. Mentors review assigned mentees and create assessments with mood, comments, action plans, and domain ratings.
  3. Admin can access the mentee directory and assignment flows for oversight.
  4. Progress reports and exports are used for tracking across the mentoring cycle.
Repository and Profiles
  1. Users upload repository assets with category, tags, file, and role-based visibility.
  2. Assets can be linked to reports, diaries, transcripts, or profile artifacts using record IDs.
  3. Volunteers manage public profile artifacts from their workspace.
  4. Admin and reviewers can inspect linked evidence and volunteer profiles during review.
Notifications and Monitoring
  1. Admin sends role-targeted notifications from Manage Notifications.
  2. The shared Notification Center shows messages relevant to the logged-in user.
  3. The system also creates notifications during review handoff and final approval outcomes.
  4. Admin uses dashboards and exports to monitor usage, assignment coverage, and reporting health.
{% elif user.role == "volunteer" %}
Your Workflow
As a volunteer, your main work is reporting programme activity, managing your profile, keeping supporting evidence, and generating transcript drafts from approved records.
Work Diary
  1. Create diary entries with date, duration, location, narrative, and optional evidence.
  2. Keep records in Draft while editing.
  3. Submit completed entries for review.
  4. If a diary is returned, update it and submit again.
Reflective Reports
  1. Submit reflective reports with programme, location, endorser, learnings, suggestions, and evidence.
  2. Draft reports remain editable until submission.
  3. Reviewed and approved reports become part of your verified activity record.
  4. Returned reports must be corrected and resubmitted.
Transcript Drafts
  1. Generate transcript drafts from approved reports, approved diaries, tracked activities, profile artifacts, and linked evidence.
  2. Submit transcript drafts for review once the content is ready.
  3. Track the status as Pending Review, Approved, or returned to Draft for correction.
  4. Download the export file after mentor or endorser approval.
Profile and Evidence
  1. Upload profile artifacts and mark public items for your volunteer public profile.
  2. Store supporting files in the repository.
  3. Link repository assets to reports, diaries, transcripts, or profile artifacts where required.
  4. Use the Notification Center to check review outcomes and follow-up actions.
{% elif user.role == "mentor" %}
Your Workflow
As a mentor, you manage assigned mentees, record assessments, and review volunteer submissions that reach your queue.
Mentee Management
  1. Open My Mentees to view assigned mentees.
  2. Review objective items, year plans, and assessment history for each mentee.
  3. Create assessments with session type, mood, remarks, action plans, and ratings.
  4. Use these records to track development and follow-up conversations.
Volunteer Review
  1. Use Review Dashboard and Review Queue for volunteer diaries, reflective reports, and transcript drafts in scope.
  2. Review submitted items and either progress them forward or return them for correction.
  3. Approved transcript drafts are published immediately after your review.
  4. Notifications and approval logs are created as part of each review action.
Activity Tracking
  1. Use My Activities to monitor mentoring work already recorded in the application.
  2. Keep assignment-linked records current through assessments and related follow-up.
  3. Use DIP / YCLP pages where programme-specific workflow is assigned to you.
  4. Coordinate with admin when assignment or data setup needs correction.
Shared Tools
  1. Use the repository to inspect supporting evidence and shared materials.
  2. Open the Notification Center for review-related alerts.
  3. Use the Workflow Guide for role-specific steps while your active role is mentor.
  4. Switch roles from the header when you need to work in another assigned role.
{% elif user.role == "endorser" %}
Your Workflow
As an endorser, you oversee assigned mentors, maintain your schedule, and review volunteer submissions in your scope.
Assigned Mentors
  1. Use Assigned Mentors to view the mentors mapped under your responsibility.
  2. Review related activity when you need mentor-level visibility.
  3. Maintain your work schedule in the endorser workspace.
  4. Coordinate escalations with admin where operational changes are needed.
Review Queue
  1. Use Review Dashboard and Review Queue to process volunteer reports, diaries, and transcripts in your scope.
  2. Move complete items forward after review or return them when correction is needed.
  3. Approved transcript drafts are published immediately after your review.
  4. Use notifications and the audit trail to track review decisions.
Transcript Review
  1. Review generated transcript drafts against the volunteer’s approved records and linked evidence.
  2. Confirm that the summary is ready for approval and export.
  3. Approve the draft to publish the transcript and make the export available to admin.
  4. Use comments and return actions when the draft needs rework.
Shared Tools
  1. Use the repository to inspect shared files and review evidence.
  2. Use the Notification Center for review handoff and status updates.
  3. Keep your profile information updated in the endorser shell.
  4. Switch roles from the header if you have more than one assigned role.
{% elif user.role == "mentee" %}
Your Workflow
As a mentee, your work is focused on planning, keeping your progress records current, and reviewing mentor feedback across your development journey.
Objectives
  1. Create and update objective items for your development goals.
  2. Maintain progress details, remarks, and supporting evidence where applicable.
  3. Use objectives as the base for structured mentoring follow-up.
  4. Review mentor feedback attached to your records.
Year Plans
  1. Maintain year-wise plans with dates, progress notes, and evidence.
  2. Keep the records current so mentors can review your progress clearly.
  3. Use the year plan as the timeline view of your developmental journey.
  4. Update items after meetings or agreed action points.
Assessments
  1. Review assessment history recorded by your mentor.
  2. Track remarks, mood capture, action plans, and domain ratings over time.
  3. Use the assessment history to understand strengths, concerns, and follow-up areas.
  4. Export progress information when required by the programme.
Shared Tools
  1. Use the repository for shared materials and supporting files available to your role.
  2. Open the Notification Center for updates related to your records.
  3. Keep your profile information current.
  4. Use the Workflow Guide as a mentee-specific reference while this role is active.
